Academic Support

We offer a range of academic support services to help students achieve their goals and succeed in their studies.

Academic Advising

Our academic advisors can help you plan your course of study, choose your major, and set academic goals.

Tutoring Services

We offer one-on-one tutoring in a variety of subjects, as well as group tutoring sessions and study groups.

Technology Support

Our technology support team can assist you with software installation, troubleshooting, and other technical issues.

Writing Center

Our writing center offers support for all aspects of the writing process, from brainstorming to revising. Our writing tutors can help students improve their writing skills and gain confidence in their writing abilities.

Study Groups

Our study groups provide students with a supportive and collaborative environment in which to study and learn. Students can form their own study groups or join one of our pre-existing groups.

Technology Services

Computer Labs

Our computer labs are equipped with the latest hardware and software to support a wide range of academic programs. Our labs are open 24/7 and are staffed with knowledgeable technicians to help students with any technical issues.

  • Windows and Macintosh computers
  • Specialized software for engineering, design, and multimedia
  • Printing and scanning capabilities
  • High-speed internet access

Online Learning

We offer a wide range of online learning resources to support students and faculty. Our learning management system provides access to course materials, discussion forums, and assignments from anywhere with an internet connection.

  • 24/7 access to course materials
  • Collaboration tools for group projects
  • Discussion forums for peer support
  • Online quizzes and assessments
